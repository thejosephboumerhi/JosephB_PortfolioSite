Game Development: Project “Homunculus” (In Progress)

Realizing the concept of Project “Homunculus” was a turning point for me in terms of expanding my curiosity for a plethora of new interests. The main theme, or the research question I wondered was “how do we find purpose in a sublimating world by AI?”. This question was conceived as a result of the ever-accelerating growth of AI and concerns to most of us, a prominent one being pessimistic as different actors which to push it forward over human workers. While making it within a class too ambitious for its time frame, I had gotten really promising propositions and questions from some peers and the instructors on “how to try to convince people of the idea”, which is an idea I've been taking to heart recently.

While it is an “in-progress” thing, I found the conception of this work to be a motivating force for me to discover new ways of thought, an opportunity to really get into new tools to tinker with. As a thing I'm searching for is to embody the inhabitants of this game's world in different ideologies, so far I've been thinking of factions based on intellectualism, hedonism, and nihilism.
